Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to advertise and publicise for the whitewater rafting company, and to provide enough information for a customer to want to book an experience.

Audience

Who is the target audience? Young people looking for a thrilling get away and to learn new skills. What do they want? A new experience full of thrills and physical exertion. What do they exxpect to find on the site? Information and prices on different experiences, including difficulty and level of experience required, any equipment, dangers, and other disclaimers involved.
